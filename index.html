About Paper Chef

Transforming home cooking through intelligent meal planning

Our Story

Paper Chef was born from a simple frustration: the endless cycle of meal planning, grocery shopping, and wondering what to cook with ingredients already at home. Founded in 2020 by a team of food technologists, professional chefs, and AI researchers, we set out to solve this universal problem. After years of development and testing with thousands of home cooks, we created a platform that understands your ingredients, knows what tastes great, and creates personalized meal plans in seconds. Today, Paper Chef serves over 100,000 users worldwide, helping families save time, reduce food waste, and discover new favorite recipes.

What started as a passion project in a small kitchen has grown into a mission to revolutionize how people approach cooking and meal planning. We've partnered with Michelin-starred chefs to curate our recipe collection, invested in cutting-edge AI technology to perfect our OCR capabilities, and built a community of food enthusiasts who believe cooking should be joyful, not stressful.

Our Mission & Values

At Paper Chef, our mission is simple: to make cooking accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable for everyone. We believe that great meals shouldn't require hours of planning or shopping at multiple stores. We're committed to reducing food waste, promoting healthier eating habits, and helping people discover the joy of cooking with ingredients they already have. Our core values—innovation, sustainability, and community—guide every decision we make, from the recipes we feature to the technology we develop.

We envision a world where every home cook has access to personalized meal planning powered by AI and inspired by professional chefs. By combining technology with culinary expertise, we're building a platform that grows smarter with every meal you cook, every recipe you try, and every preference you share. Join us in our mission to transform the way the world cooks.
